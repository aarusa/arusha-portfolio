My Journey to Salesforce Marketing Cloud

By Arusha Shahi March 2026

When I first heard about Marketing Cloud, I honestly thought it was just another digital marketing tool. Coming from a software engineering background, I didn't pay much attention to it.

Marketing felt like a completely different field, and I didn't see how it fit into my career. But after facing multiple failed attempts to land a job in software engineering, I decided to give Marketing Cloud a chance.

I started with the basics—learning simple terms and understanding what the platform actually does. At that point, I was still not fully convinced. Everything changed when I finally got access to the system. With the help of my mentor, I was able to get a subscription and start exploring it hands-on.

"Salesforce Marketing Cloud is not just about marketing—it’s a powerful platform with complex technical layers."

Under my mentor’s guidance (a professional cloud developer), I began to understand the architecture much faster. I realized the potential for custom development, API integrations, and complex data logic.

I seriously started learning Salesforce Marketing Cloud in February 2026. Since getting access, I’ve been learning and practicing every day. Recently, I also started learning through Trailhead, which has helped me strengthen my fundamentals.

So far, I’ve covered the basics of Email Studio, Mobile Studio, CloudPages, Content Builder, Contact Builder, Automations, Journeys, and even started building custom SFMC tools. My next goal is to take the Email Specialist certification next month, and then move on to becoming a Marketing Cloud Developer.

Looking back, I never thought I would shift towards something like this. But with the right guidance and consistent effort, things started to make sense. This is just the beginning of my journey, and I’m excited to see where it takes me.
